## What Is This?

The DeNiro Zone is a fully-functional web application suite featuring nine interactive calculators and trackers built by a grumpy Robert DeNiro who was coerced into web development against his will.

This website was created through a series of increasingly ridiculous conversations where the user kept asking for one more app, and I kept grumbling about it while building them anyway.

## The Applications

### 1. **Honduras Pretzel Price Tracker**
Monitor the fluctuating prices of pretzels across Honduras because apparently that's what the internet needed. Includes city breakdowns, vendor type analysis, and price trends that are about as accurate as my memory of 2001.

### 2. **DeNiro Mortality Calculator**
Determine how much longer you're going to live based on your age, health status, stress level, and years spent complaining. Results are about as accurate as a weather forecast made by someone who just guesses.

### 3. **Paraguay Empanada Humidity Tracker**
Monitor empanada humidity levels across Paraguay. Because food science is apparently what we're tracking now. Includes a comprehensive quality index explaining why certain humidity levels produce soggy vs. crispy empanadas.

### 4. **Joe Pesci Child Support Calculator**
A satirical compound interest calculator tracking the mounting debt Joe Pesci allegedly owes after 34+ years of non-payment. Updated with annual interest rates because even fictional child support compounds.

### 5. **Southern Hemisphere Clock**
Four randomly selected cities from the southern hemisphere displaying current local time. Completely pointless. You don't need to know what time it is in Wellington. Now you do anyway.

### 6. **English-to-English Translator**
Translates English to English in five different tones:
- Unnecessarily Pompous
- Aggressively Casual  
- Old Man Yelling at Cloud
- Pretentious Film Critic
- For Some Reason, Pirate

It accomplishes nothing except making you say the same thing in a different way.

### 7. **Kiefer Sutherland DUI Counter**
A real-time (fictional) tracker of Kiefer Sutherland's DUI stops this week. Entirely made up. Totally false. But entertaining nonetheless.

### 8. **Charlie Sheen ASCII Art**
Pure ASCII art representation of Charlie Sheen as Rick "Wild Thing" Vaughn from Major League. Made entirely of keyboard characters for maximum nostalgia.

### 9. **Robert DeNiro's Bedtime Is**
An unnecessarily colorful, full-width, neon-flashing display of what time you should be in bed. Features rainbow-cycling text colors and dramatic neon glow effects. Takes up an entire row for no real reason. This is the app that finally broke me.

## Technical Details

- **Animations**: Neon flashing, color cycling, fade-ins, and other unnecessary flourishes
